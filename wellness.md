# Tailored Wellness Sessions @ CPAL

On day 2 of the conference (Tuesday, March 24), as part of the conference
program there will be a tailored wellness session from 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM.

The session will be led by a specialist in the field of organizational
development who focuses on tailoring mindfulness tools for professionals.
The sessions will be oriented specifically for supporting researchers in
computational disciplines.

We are offering this opportunity as a way to help ensure your experience at
CPAL is not only informative and engaging, but uplifting as well.
**All are welcome to attend!** No special clothing, equipment, etc. is required.
