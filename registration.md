# Accommodations

Please note that we have reserved a limited number of hotel rooms at special
conference rates. See the relevant [travel page]({{ site.baseurl }}/hotels/) for
registration details. Please be sure to submit your information **before 16
February 2026**, as the number of rooms available is limited.

# Student Discounts

Students can receive a discount of 40% off the regular ticket price. To receive
the discount, upload a valid proof of student status during your registration.
See the [registration page](https://cpal2026.vokdams-ticketservice.de/payment/start) for more details.

# Registration Help
